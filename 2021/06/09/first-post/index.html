Blogs are dead.

I tried this new-fangled idea of “web-logging” my thoughts once back when WordPress was a new thing, but I found that, unless you’re one of the microscopic percentile of opinionated pundits or enlightened tech-gurus that has a following, it’s just a huge waste of time and effort for what ultimately amounts to a public diary which nobody will ever read.

So, yeah, here I am bloggin’ again just to make the point.
