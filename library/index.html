Community

I always liked the concept that Communication + Unity = Community, but haven’t meditated on why.

In my experience, a neighborhood is healthy when its community of families is open and unified, and I see cities as larger, frankensteined patchwork structures of the more human-scaled neighborhoods. I think that a healthy city would require the same collective, open and transparent communication operating under a peaceful, unified effort.

I can see the nation-state as a next-order amalgam of these frankeneighborhood cities
and that if the same community principles of open communication and cooperative unity were applied at this level the nation might just survive and thrive.
